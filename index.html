TrndStar

Client: OVER Media INC. / TrndStar
Date: November 2015 - Present

TrndStar aims to bring new features to the social media environment. Currently, with no good environment for new upcoming artists to make a name for themselves, TrndStar plans to put these upcomers right in the middle of the action. Users can gain a following by uploading videos, competing, and collaborating. TrndStar features the ability to upload 90 second videos and gain exposure as a new artist or entertainer. Users can challenge one another and have their videos seen by both users followers. While multiple users can collaborate and create one video to attract as many followers as possible.
